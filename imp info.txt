We apply taxes to Subscriptions (and Waitlist purchases) for recipients in California, North Carolina, and New York. The rates are generally ~8%, but vary by zip code.
For Gifts, we charge the PURCHASER the tax (see above), if the recipient lives in one of those 3 states.
Canadians get charged a flat rate of $14.95 (i.e. $4.95 in shipping), USD.
